Interact with the image to reveal additional information.

Cornea

Light enters the eye through the cornea and is refracted towards the pupil.

Lens

The lens brings light into focus.

Retina

A layer of tissue containing photoreceptors; cells which are receptive to light.

Pupil

The iris controls the size of the pupil which in turn controls the amount of light that reaches the lens.

Fovea

A point in the retina where focused light produces the clearest image.

Cones

Receptive to dim light.

Rods

Receptive to bright light.
